Financial Aid

Need-Based Assistance

Supporting Our Students

UAC is committed to making quality education accessible to all qualified students. Our need-based financial aid program helps bridge the gap between what families can afford and the cost of education.

Application Process

How to Apply

Simple Steps to Apply

Our financial aid application process is straightforward and confidential. We work with families to understand their financial situation and provide appropriate assistance based on demonstrated need.

Types of Aid

Available Assistance

Comprehensive Support

We offer various types of financial assistance, including tuition grants, payment plans, and emergency aid. Our goal is to ensure that financial constraints don't prevent qualified students from attending UAC.
