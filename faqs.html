Frequently Asked Questions

Find Your Answers

What makes UAC different from other schools?

UAC combines rigorous academic standards with Islamic values, creating a unique educational environment that nurtures both intellectual growth and spiritual development. Our curriculum integrates traditional subjects with Islamic studies, fostering well-rounded individuals who excel academically while maintaining strong moral values.

What are the admission requirements?

Our admission process includes an application form, academic records review, student assessment, and family interview. We evaluate each student holistically, considering academic potential, character, and family commitment to Islamic values. Specific requirements vary by grade level.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain a low student-teacher ratio of approximately 15:1, ensuring personalized attention and support for each student. This allows our teachers to better understand individual learning needs and provide targeted assistance when required.

What extracurricular activities are available?

UAC offers a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports, arts, robotics, debate, Quran competitions, and community service programs. These activities are designed to develop leadership skills, foster creativity, and build character while maintaining Islamic values.

How does UAC support students' Islamic development?

Our Islamic development program includes daily prayers, Quran studies, Islamic history, and character education. We integrate Islamic values throughout the curriculum and provide opportunities for spiritual growth through various activities and community service projects.

What are the school hours and calendar?

School hours are from 8:00 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday through Friday. We follow a traditional academic calendar with breaks for major Islamic holidays, winter break, and summer vacation. Extended care is available before and after school hours.

What support services are available for students?

UAC provides comprehensive support services including academic counseling, learning support, social-emotional counseling, and college preparation. Our dedicated staff works closely with students and families to ensure each student reaches their full potential.
